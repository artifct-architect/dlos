# DLOS — Example Use Cases

## Archival Preservation

Cultural and historical artifacts can retain authorship and lineage
across generations without dependence on a single system.

---

## Research Artifacts

Academic work can be published as self-verifying objects with traceable
derivation and version history.

---

## Digital Identity Records

Important personal or institutional records can persist across systems
without re-verification cycles.

---

## Cross-Platform Assets

Digital objects (media, designs, models) can move across platforms while
retaining identity and provenance.

---

## Long-Term Storage

Artifacts remain verifiable decades later without requiring the original
platform.
